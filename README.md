## 📖 Project Overview

This repository contains a **complete Information Security Management System (ISMS)** designed and documented in alignment with **ISO/IEC 27001:2022**.

The ISMS was built for a simulated organisation — **PrimeHealth Clinic & Diagnostics**, an Australian healthcare provider with a turnover of $800M, more than 1,000 healthcare practitioners, and 40 branch locations across multiple states. The project demonstrates how the ISO 27001 standard is applied end-to-end to a real-world business context, including healthcare-specific regulatory obligations (Privacy Act 1988, HRIP Act 2002, ACSC Essential Eight, NPAAC, NSQHS, and others).

> ⚠️ **Disclaimer:** PrimeHealth Clinic & Diagnostics is a fictional company created for academic and portfolio purposes. All data, names, and figures are illustrative.

---

## 🎯 Objectives of the Project

- Apply the full ISO/IEC 27001:2022 lifecycle to a real-world business scenario.
- Identify, assess, and treat information security risks following ISO 27005 methodology.
- Map applicable controls from **Annex A (ISO 27001:2022)** through a Statement of Applicability.
- Demonstrate governance, risk, and compliance (GRC) capabilities suitable for an enterprise environment.
- Produce a complete, audit-ready evidence pack covering all mandatory ISMS clauses (4 – 10).

## 📁 Repository Structure

The project follows the **clause structure of ISO/IEC 27001:2022** — making it easy for an auditor or reviewer to navigate.

```
ISO27001-ISMS-PrimeHealth/
│
├── 01_Context/                       → Clause 4: Context of the Organisation
├── 02_Leadership/                    → Clause 5: Leadership
├── 03_Risk_Management/               → Clause 6: Planning
├── 04_Support/                       → Clause 7: Support
├── 05_Operations/                    → Clause 8: Operation
└── 06_Performance_and_Improvement/   → Clauses 9 & 10: Performance Evaluation & Improvement
```

## 📄 Documentation Index

### 🔹 Clause 4 — Context of the Organisation
| # | Document | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| 01 | Company Background & Org Structure | Establishes the company, its mission, and roles & responsibilities of the executive team. |
| 02 | Standards, Frameworks & Regulations | Identifies 18+ mandatory and optional standards (ISO 27001, ISO 27002, ISO 27005, Privacy Act 1988, HRIP Act, ACSC Essential Eight, NSQHS, NPAAC, etc.). |
| 03 | ISMS Scope Statement | Defines the boundaries of the ISMS — assets, locations, departments, and processes covered. |

### 🔹 Clause 5 — Leadership
| # | Document | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| 05 | Information Security Policy | The top-level policy approved by executive management, setting strategic direction for the ISMS. |

### 🔹 Clause 6 — Planning
| # | Document | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| 06 | Information Security Risk Assessment | Risk identification, analysis, and evaluation methodology. |
| 07 | Risk Treatment Plan | Treatment options selected for each identified risk (modify / retain / avoid / share). |
| 07B | Statement of Applicability (SoA) | Annex A control mapping with inclusion/exclusion justifications. |
| 08 | Information Security Objectives & Plans | Measurable security objectives aligned with business goals. |

### 🔹 Clause 7 — Support
| # | Document | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| 07C | Security Awareness Procedure | Training & awareness programme for personnel. |
| 09 | Competence | Competence requirements for ISMS roles. |
| 09B | RASCI Matrix | Roles and responsibilities for ISMS activities. |

### 🔹 Clause 8 — Operation
| # | Document | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| 10 | Operational Planning & Control | Operational procedures for executing the ISMS. |
| 11 | Business Processes, Information Assets & Risks Register | Comprehensive register linking processes → assets → risks → controls. |
| 11B | Business Processes Documentation | Narrative documentation supporting the register. |
| 12 | Risk Treatment Results | Outcome of applied risk treatments. |

### 🔹 Clauses 9 & 10 — Performance Evaluation & Improvement
| # | Document | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| 13 | Metrics | KPIs and KRIs used to measure ISMS effectiveness. |
| 14 | ISMS Internal Audits | Internal audit programme, findings & follow-up. |
| 15 | ISMS Management Review | Quarterly executive review process. |
| 16 | Non-Conformities & Corrective Actions Register | Tracking of nonconformities and CAPA. |
| 17 | BIA & BCP Register | Business Impact Analysis and Business Continuity Plan (Annex A.17). |
